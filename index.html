Common Questions

How fast will I receive quotes?

Most homeowners receive their first quote within 24–48 hours of submitting their project details. Timing can vary depending on your location and project type.

Do you handle my insurance claim?

No — OtterQuote does not file or manage your insurance claim. What we do is help you get competitive contractor quotes once your claim has been filed, so you're not navigating the contractor search on your own.

Who sees my personal information?

We never sell or share your contact information with contractors, advertisers, or third parties. The only people who see your information are the OtterQuote team and the contractor you select.

What if I'm not happy with my contractor?

You can switch to another OtterQuote contractor at no cost, as long as it's more than 3 days before your installation date. We handle the transition — you don't have to manage it yourself.

Does OtterQuote work for my type of project?

Yes. OtterQuote works for insurance claims (whether your policy covers full replacement cost or a depreciated amount) and for projects you're paying out of pocket. If you're not sure which applies to you, we'll help you figure it out.

How do contractors pay? Is there a catch?

OtterQuote is completely free for homeowners. Contractors pay a small percentage-based fee only when they earn your business. That's the model — no hidden fees, no subscriptions, no surprises for you.
